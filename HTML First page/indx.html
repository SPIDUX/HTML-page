I'll Let You in on a Secret: My First Love

Movies have been my first love since I laid eyes on my first one: 7 Lucky Ninjas, followed by Shaolin Soccer and The Mask. I remember the days when I would pay to watch movies at a video shop in our small rural town. Back then, we used video cassettes—there was no Netflix and chill, and the internet was still years away from being a reality.

The Great Wall of Asia

Luckily, we owned one of those all-time classic Great Wall TVs powered by the galaxy, and I would watch Sinbad and Walker, Texas Ranger on Fridays. I also enjoyed WWE, where The Rock (Dwayne Johnson) would wrestle against Stone Cold Steve Austin—it was so much fun!

Movies have remained one of my top sources of entertainment, thanks to Hollywood and its great list of actors. Legends like Chuck Norris, Jackie Chan, Jet Li, Rambo, and Jean-Claude Van Damme have made a lasting impact on me, and the list of great actors keeps growing.
